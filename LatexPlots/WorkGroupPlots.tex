\section{Work Group}

Work group of 32x32 (since 8x8 creates to many data points). A work group is a 32 x 32 chunk of rays and we calculate the average, max and variance for leafs and nodes. The graphs below show how the max intersections and standard deviation correspond.

The dots for the node graph have an opacity of 0.2 and for the leaf graph an opacity of 0.1.

\pgfplotsset{
	every axis/.append style={colorbar = false},
}

	ylabel = standard deviation,

	legend style={at={(0.05,0.95)}, anchor = north west},
